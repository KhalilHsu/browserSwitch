How it works

Set the default browser once, then let BrowserRouter do the routing.

1

Install and open

Install the app, open it once, and follow onboarding to make BrowserRouter the default browser on macOS.

2

Choose your targets

Pick the browser or profile for your default fallback, then add rules for specific domains or paths.

3

Click links normally

Open links from Mail, Slack, Notes, or anywhere else. BrowserRouter sends them to the right place.
